My dream is to combine my passion for space exploration with my knowledge in rocket fluid systems to make a difference in spaceflight.

With GE Aviation, I tested fuel systems components of turbofan and turboshaft engines, and with Physical Sciences, I built and operated testbeds for advanced experimental rocket engines. As BU Rocket Propulsion Group's lead fluid systems engineer, I led the design of the liquid rocket feed system and co-designed the gas generator system. With SpaceX, I'll develop combustion devices for an interplanetary-capable rocket engine, and with ABL, I will operate launch and test systems to develop a high-cadence, low-cost launch vehicle.

About Me

I was born in New York City to my parents who immigrated from Hong Kong, I'm currently 21, and I live between Boston and New York. I'm a first-generation undergraduate student attending Boston University to major in mechanical engineering with a concentration in aerospace engineering. After I graduate in May 2022, I intend to pursue my passion for rocket science.

"Mankind was born on Earthâ€” it was never meant to die here." What happens when Earth is no longer sustainable? Our future is in interstellar exploration, and it's my dream to use my knowledge to develop modern launch vehicle technology as the NewSpace industry grows.

As a child I was enamored with reading about the universe and drawing from my overactive imagination, and as a teenager I made a hobby out of building paper crafts and designing model rockets. At different points when I was young, I thought I wanted to be an auto mechanic or an artist or an astronomer. I realized in high school that technology, design, and space all converge into aerospace engineering. Now I've experienced operating propulsion systems in industry and building liquid rockets with BURPG. Subconsciously when I was younger and actively now, my life always pointed to a calling to engineering space vehicles.

Outside of engineering, my creative interests include music, drawing, and stargazing. I enjoy exercising by running through cities I explore and hiking home in the Hudson Valley. Traveling is another outlet of my curiosity, and with my travels comes an adventure to find good food everywhere. I love learning more about our world through new experiences, and using these experiences to help others and shape the future.

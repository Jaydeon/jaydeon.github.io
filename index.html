About Me



I am a 16 year old high school senior currently living in the Hudson Valley region of New York. I was born in New York City on October 14, although my parents are both from Hong Kong, China, making me of Asian descent.

In my free time, I like to draw, both using a pencil on paper and using a graphic tablet on a computer. I also enjoy listening to various genres of music, and can play music myself through the clarinet. I enjoy exercising, especially aerobic activities such as running, biking, and swimming. When all else fails, I simply enjoy helping out whoever I can and educating myself through the Internet.

I am very academically driven. I am in the top 10% of my class of about 800, with a GPA of 99.89. I have taken numbers of rigorous honors and Advanced Placement courses, notably AP World History, AP Language and Composition, and AP US History and Government. I have done a wide variety of extracurricular activities at my school related to music, French, and newspaper. I also volunteer in my local community at both the library and the animal shelter, having over 50 hours accumulated.

When I go to college, I aspire to major in mechanical engineering for a bachelor's degree at a four-year private university, and then further specialize in aerospace engineering for a master's degree. I am absolutely fascinated by the unknowns of space, and I would love to one day send mankind into space, permanently.

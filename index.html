About Me

I am a 20 year old student residing in the Hudson Valley of upstate New York. Born in New York City to my parents from Hong Kong, I'm a second-generation Chinese-American and a first-generation college student. As a rising senior I am attending Boston University in Massachusetts to study Mechanical Engineering with a Concentration in Aerospace Engineering, and I will graduate in May 2022.

My interests outside of engineering include playing and listening to music, drawing art, and night sky watching for constellations, meteor showers, and planets.

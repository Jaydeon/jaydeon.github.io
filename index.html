About Me



I am a 20 year old student residing in the Hudson Valley of upstate New York. Born in New York City to my parents from Hong Kong, I'm a second-generation Chinese-American and a first-generation college student. As a rising senior I am attending Boston University in Massachusetts to study for my Bachelor's of Science in Mechanical Engineering with a Concentration in Aerospace Engineering, and I will graduate in May 2022.

My interests outside of engineering include playing and listening to music, drawing art, and night sky watching for constellations, meteor showers, and planets. I stay active by running around the cities I explore, hiking at home in the Hudson Valley, and playing basketball with my friends. I enjoy learning more about the world through new experiences, and using these experiences to mentor and help others around me.

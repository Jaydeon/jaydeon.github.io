Engine Development

In Summer 2021, after designing the pressurization and feed system for BURPG's liquid bipropellant rocket Pursuit, I gained a great deal of expertise and interest in vehicle high-pressure fluid systems. While I started to assemble and test the feed system, I wished to also supplement my fluids knowledge with a personal project in propulsion. My work with the chamber of Pursuit's gas generator gave me exposure to propulsion, but as I was not responsible for the overall system, I lacked experience in injector, nozzle, and cooling design. My goal was to design a liquid rocket engine by the end of summer, with the constraint that the system could be reasonably tested using college resources.

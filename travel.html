Travel Journal

Who doesn't love traveling? I've had the fortunate opportunity to see 16 different countries in my lifetime, and I plan on expanding my world view more as I grow up. Some travel highlights include New York and Boston, my home cities that I know like the back of my hand at this point- New York is the bustling city where there's never a shortage of things to do, while Boston is the seaside city that has my favorite scenic running and walking routes. Chicago is my favorite U.S. city for a contrast of technologically advanced and industrially historic vibes and probably the best unique food I've ever had in any state- italian beef and deep dish pizza.

As for countries, Hong Kong is special in my heart for being the root of my family, but also for authentic and cheap Cantonese cuisine- dim sum, wonton noodles, cha chaang teng, hot pot, roast duck, pineapple buns, egg waffles, HK milk tea to name only a few. The islands of Greece have the most gorgeous views in the world- blue and white also happen to be my favorite colors- and inexpensive, delicious large portions of gyro and souflaki. Australia is special for being my study abroad; Sydney has a beautiful coastal city layout, the friendliest and most chill people I've met, and the greatest beaches for surfing.

True to my space nerdiness though, my dream vacation is one that's out of this world. If Earth is this diverse and impressive, who knows what's waiting for us in outer space?
